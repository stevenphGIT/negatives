What is Negatives?

Negatives is a game about double negatives. It tests your ability to read quickly and accurately. Let's see an example:


Prompt: Do not click the Red button.

This prompt is fairly simple. Click any button except for the red button.

Prompt: Do not not click the Red button.

This is where it gets a little more tricky. Here, you MUST click the red button, as there is a double negative.

Note that, unless the prompt says to do nothing, a button must always be pressed.
